Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters, level up, and become stronger in this intense action survival game!

How to Play

  • - Move using WASD or arrow keys
  • - Automatically attack nearby monsters
  • - Collect experience orbs to level up
  • - Choose new abilities when leveling up
  • - Survive as long as possible

Game Features

  • - Multiple character abilities
  • - Progressive difficulty
  • - Various enemy types
  • - Powerups and upgrades
  • - Endless gameplay

Tips for Success

Keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters. Choose your abilities wisely as they will determine your survival strategy. Focus on collecting experience orbs to level up quickly and become stronger. Some ability combinations work better than others - experiment to find the best build for your playstyle.
